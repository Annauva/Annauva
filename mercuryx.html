mercuryxofficial.com

Mercury X is a metal band that wanted a website that fits their style and showcases their music in a nice way. I chose to have a big video that catches the visitors attention and have the most important bits on the front page, like a spotify playlist and a featured video. And of course the contact info so that they can be booked for gigs!
I made the site into a wordpress theme so that they easily can update the content on the site. I also made the site responsive so that it looks great on smaller devices too.
